Hi! My name is Alia Jiwani I am a Yoga Teacher and Breathwork Facilitator.

I have been practicing Yoga for the past 10 years and have a background in Psychology and Neuroscience.

I am passionate about sharing my knowledge and experience of yoga and holistic health to help unlock our bodies' ability to heal itself.

Yoga inspires me to be more mindful in my everyday life and I am privileged to be able to share these learnings with the people around me.

Private Yoga

$ 65 /hour

Private yoga is a great way to get going for a variety of reasons. If you’re nervous to join a group class setting, private yoga can offer you an extra level of comfort and ease. If you want to gain a better understanding of your practice as a new or experienced student, private yoga can give you the opportunity to ask questions while you practice and get personalized feedback. If you’re looking for a private class for a group of friends, for a special occasion, or a team building activity at work, private yoga can be a great way to bring people together.

Group Yoga

$ 150 /hour

Group classes are now offered via Zoom! If you are new to Yoga or are continuing your journey, these classes are a great way to support your mind + body. Restorative classes offer a meditative practice where we hold poses up to 5 minutes, supporting the body as we calm the nervous system and allow for deep stretching and relaxation. Our Hatha classes offer a gentle practice that balances effort with ease as we move through physical postures guided by our breath. Accessible and affordable for all levels of students.
