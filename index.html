Hi! My name is Alia Jiwani I am a Yoga Teacher and Breathwork Facilitator.

I have been practicing Yoga for the past 10 years and have a background in Psychology and Neuroscience. I am passionate about sharing my knowledge and experience of yoga and holistic health to help unlock our bodies' ability to heal itself. Yoga inspires me to be more mindful in my everyday life and I am privileged to be able to share these learnings with the people around me.
